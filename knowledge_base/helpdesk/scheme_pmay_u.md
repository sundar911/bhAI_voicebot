# Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U)

## Keywords
PMAY, PMAY-U, Awas Yojana, आवास योजना, housing scheme, घर subsidy, EWS housing, LIG, MIG, pucca house, slum rehabilitation, home loan subsidy

## Overview
Subsidized pucca houses for urban poor under "Housing for All by 2022".

## Key Features
- Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) for home loans
- Aadhaar mandatory for all beneficiaries

## Components
- *EWS (Income ≤ ₹3L)*: House ≤ 30 sq.m
- *LIG (₹3-6L)*: House ≤ 60 sq.m
- *MIG I (₹6-12L)*: House ≤ 160 sq.m
- *MIG II (₹12-18L)*: House ≤ 200 sq.m
- Slum Rehabilitation through private developers
- Beneficiary-Led Construction/Enhancement for EWS

## Eligibility
- Belongs to EWS/LIG/MIG I/MIG II
- No pucca house owned by any family member across India
- Resides in PMAY-covered town/city
- No prior govt housing scheme availed
- Family = Husband + Wife + Unmarried Children

## Required Documents
Aadhaar, income proof (affidavit/certificate), ID & address proof (PAN/Voter ID), EWS/LIG/MIG certificate, salary slips/IT returns, bank details, property documents, affidavit of not owning pucca house.

## Where to Check
pmaymis.gov.in — application, subsidy status, beneficiary search.
