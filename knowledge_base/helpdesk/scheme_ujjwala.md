# Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

## Keywords
Ujjwala, उज्ज्वला, free LPG, गैस connection, gas cylinder, BPL gas, free chulha, smoke-free kitchen, deposit-free LPG

## Objective
Free LPG connection to women from poor households — replaces unhealthy chulha/kerosene cooking.

## Key Benefits
- **Free LPG connection**: deposit, regulator, hose, and first refill cost waived (Ujjwala 2.0)
- **Free first refill** + free hot plate in some categories
- **Subsidy on refills**: ₹300 subsidy per cylinder (up to 12 cylinders/year), credited to bank account

## Eligibility (Ujjwala 2.0)
- Adult woman from a poor household
- No LPG connection in any household member's name
- Belongs to: SC/ST, PMAY beneficiary, AAY, MBC, Antyodaya, forest dwellers, tea garden workers, islands & river island residents, or any BPL family per 14-point declaration

## Documents Required
Aadhaar, ration card, bank passbook, address proof, recent photo, KYC form, supplementary KYC (declaration as above).

## Where to Apply
Any LPG distributor (Indane, HP, Bharat Gas) — can also pre-fill at pmuy.gov.in.

## Where to Check
pmuy.gov.in — eligibility, distributor locator, status.
