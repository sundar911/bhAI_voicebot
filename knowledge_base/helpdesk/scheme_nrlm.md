# Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – NRLM (DAY-NRLM)

## Keywords
DAY-NRLM, NRLM, Deendayal, अंत्योदय, self-help group, SHG, बचत समूह, livelihood, rural women, gramin, RSETI, aajeevika

## Mission
Reduce rural poverty by enabling poor households to access self-employment and skilled wage employment through grassroots institutions.

## Key Features
- Target: 7 crore rural poor households across 600 districts and 6 lakh villages in 8-10 years
- Community-led, demand-driven approach
- Intensive blocks: full implementation; non-intensive: limited support

## Core Components
- Social Mobilization & Capacity Building
- Financial Inclusion
- Livelihood Promotion
- Convergence with government schemes

## Key Benefits
- One woman from each rural poor household linked to Self-Help Group (SHG)
- SHGs get bank linkages, interest subvention, credit support
- Village-level federations support SHGs
- Special focus on single women, disabled, landless
- Revolving Fund to SHGs, Community Investment Fund (CIF) to federations
- Skill training & placements through RSETIs
- Insurance (life/health) and market access support

## Eligibility for SHGs
- At least 6 months of active existence
- Must follow Panchasutras (regular meetings, savings, inter-loaning, timely repayment, updated books)
- Must meet NABARD grading norms

## Documents Required
Aadhaar number, proof of identity, proof of residence, Voter ID, passport-size photo.

## Where to Check
aajeevika.gov.in — SHG formation, livelihoods support, bank linkage.
