# Voter ID

## Common Questions & Answers

### "Who can vote?"
Any Indian citizen aged 18 or above with a valid Voter ID.

### "How to register?"
Register online at https://voters.eci.gov.in/ or visit your nearest voter centre.

### "What is EPIC No.?"
It is the unique number printed on your Voter ID card.

### "Can I vote without ID?"
No, a valid ID like Voter ID or Aadhaar is mandatory at the polling booth.

### "How to check my name in voter list?"
Check at https://voters.eci.gov.in/

### "Changed address – what to do?"
Update your address by submitting Form 8 online.

### "Lost Voter ID?"
Apply for a duplicate Voter ID through the NVSP portal.

### "Where to vote?"
Your polling booth is mentioned on your Voter ID or can be found online at the ECI portal.

### "Is voting a must?"
No, but it is a key right and responsibility in a democracy.

## Required Documents

### For New Voter ID
- **Identity Proof** (any one): Aadhaar Card, PAN Card
- **Address Proof** (any one): Electricity Bill (same address as Aadhaar), Ration Card
- **Date of Birth Proof** (any one): Birth Certificate, Aadhaar Card
- Passport size photo
- Bank passbook
- Mobile number
- Carry originals and 1 Xerox copy

### For Update/Correction
- **Identity Proof** (Name/Gender Update): Aadhaar Card, PAN Card
- **Address Proof** (Address Update): Electricity Bill, Ration Card
- **Date of Birth Proof** (DOB Update): Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate
- Carry originals and 1 Xerox copy of each document

## Steps at the Centre
Inform the officer about the service needed:
- New Voter ID
- Correction (Name, DOB, Address)
- Mobile Number Update
- Voter ID Print/Reprint
