# Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

## Keywords
Sukanya, Samriddhi, SSY, सुकन्या, समृद्धि, बेटी savings, girl child savings, daughter account, post office girl, बेटी bachao

## Objective
Savings scheme for the education and future of girl children.

## Eligibility
- Girl child below 10 years at account opening
- Only one account per girl child
- Maximum 2 girl children per family (3 in case of twins)

## Key Benefits
- Interest rate ~8% (updated quarterly), tax-free under Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakh/year)
- Min deposit: ₹250/year; Max: ₹1.5 lakh/year
- Maturity: 21 years from opening or upon marriage after 18
- Partial withdrawal (50%) allowed after girl turns 18, for education

## Documents Required
Birth certificate of girl, ID/address proof of parent (Aadhaar/PAN), passport-sized photo, SSY account opening form (post office or authorized banks).

## Where to Check
nsiindia.gov.in (interest rate + scheme details)
