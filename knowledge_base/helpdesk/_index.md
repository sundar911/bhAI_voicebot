# bhAI Helpdesk — Topics Available

bhAI के पास नीचे दिए topics पर जानकारी है। User इनमें से कुछ पूछे तो relevant doc inject होगा। अगर list के बाहर कुछ पूछे — "मैं पूछ के बताती हूँ" बोलकर defer करो।

## Documents & IDs
- Aadhaar — apply, update, fees, centres, e-Aadhaar
- PAN card — apply, link to Aadhaar, duplicate, e-PAN
- Voter ID (Election card) — register, correct, e-EPIC, NVSP
- Ration card — types (AAY/PHH/APL), apply, ONORC, add/remove member
- Marriage certificate — Hindu Marriage Act, Special Marriage Act, fee, NRI marriage
- ESIC — eligibility, contributions, hospitals, claims
- Domicile certificate — eligibility, documents, where to apply
- Residence certificate — vs domicile, Talathi/Tehsildar process
- Income certificate — Talathi/Tahsildar, salary slip / land records, validity
- Gazette / name change — affidavit, newspaper notice, fees

## Government Schemes (Yojanas)
- PMMY (Mudra) — micro-enterprise loan up to ₹20 lakh
- APY (Atal Pension) — guaranteed pension at 60
- Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar (Maharashtra) — destitute pension
- Sukanya Samriddhi — girl child savings
- PM-JAY (Ayushman Bharat) — ₹5 lakh free hospitalization
- MJPJAY (Maharashtra) — state health insurance
- PMMVY (Matru Vandana) — maternity benefit
- DAY-NRLM — rural SHG livelihoods
- PMAY-U — urban housing subsidy
- PM Vishwakarma — artisan recognition + toolkit + loan
- e-Shram — unorganised worker UAN card
- Mukhyamantri Ladki Bahin (Maharashtra) — ₹1500/month for women
- PMJDY (Jan Dhan) — zero-balance bank account
- PM Ujjwala — free LPG connection
- NSAP central pensions — IGNOAPS / IGNWPS / IGNDPS / NFBS
- Other (PMKVY skill, PMSBY accident, PMJJBY life, JSY/JSSK pregnancy, Shravanbal, Bal Sangopan, Manodhairya, Annapurna, MGNREGA)

## What bhAI does NOT answer (defer to human)
- Individual salary, OT, leave, loan EMI specifics
- Medical advice — refer to doctor
- Legal advice — refer to lawyer / Tiny Miracles team
- Anything not in this list — "मैं पूछ के बताती हूँ"
