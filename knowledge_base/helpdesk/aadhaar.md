# Aadhaar Card

## Common Questions & Answers

### "How to update Aadhaar?"
You can update name, DOB, gender, address, mobile number, email, photo, and biometrics by visiting an Aadhaar Seva Kendra. Only address updates can be done online.

### "Can I update Aadhaar online?"
Only address changes can be updated online. All other updates require visiting the Aadhaar Enrolment Centre.

### "What documents are needed for update?"
- **Name Change**: Any valid Proof of Identity (PAN, Passport, Voter ID)
- **DOB Correction**: Birth Certificate, Passport, or SSLC certificate
- **Address Change**: Any valid Proof of Address (utility bill, bank statement, driving license)
- **Mobile Number/Email Update**: No documents required, just OTP verification

### "How long does an update take?"
Usually 7 to 90 days, depending on the type of update.

### "What is the fee for Aadhaar change?"
- Demographic Updates (Name, DOB, Gender, Address, Email, Mobile): ₹50
- Biometric Update (Photo, Fingerprints, Iris): ₹100
- Aadhaar PVC card: ₹50

### "Can I re-update Aadhaar details?"
Yes, but with limits:
- Name: Maximum twice in a lifetime
- DOB: Only once (DOB mismatch over 3 years needs extra proof)
- Gender: Maximum once in a lifetime

### "What if I lost my Aadhaar?"
You can download e-Aadhaar from the UIDAI website using your Aadhaar number or enrolment ID.

### "Aadhaar update rejected?"
Could be due to incorrect documents or mismatched information. Try again with correct documents or visit the Aadhaar Seva Kendra for help.

### "How to link Aadhaar to PAN?"
Linking is mandatory for tax filing. Do it on the Income Tax portal. For bank linking, visit your bank.

### "Is my mobile number linked?"
Visit UIDAI's website → Aadhaar Services → Verify Aadhaar Number to check.

## Required Documents

### For New Aadhaar Card
- **Identity Proof** (any one): PAN Card, Voter ID
- **Address Proof** (any one): Electricity Bill, Bank Passbook
- **Date of Birth Proof** (any one): Birth Certificate, SSC Certificate (10th Marksheet)
- Carry originals along with 1 Xerox copy of each document

### For Aadhaar Update/Correction
- **Identity Proof** (for Name/Gender Update): PAN Card, Voter ID
- **Address Proof** (for Address Update): Electricity Bill, Bank Passbook
- **Date of Birth Proof** (for DOB Update): Birth Certificate, PAN Card (if DOB mentioned)
- Carry originals and 1 Xerox copy of each document

## Aadhaar Centre Visit – Step by Step (Ground Work)

### 1. Subah jaldi centre par pahunchna
Aadhaar centre subah 9 ya 10 baje se kaam shuru karte hain. Log subah 7-8 baje se hi line me lag jaate hain — pehle aane walon ko jaldi number milta hai. Late jaane par aapka number door aata hai ya same din kaam nahi ho pata.

### 2. Token/Serial number lena
Centre wale ek token number ya serial slip dete hain. Ye slip aapki turn decide karti hai. Kahin register me entry hota hai, kahin printed slip milti hai.

### 3. Kaam ka type batana
Apna number aane par batana hota hai:
- Naya Aadhaar
- Correction (naam, DOB, address, etc.)
- Mobile number update
- Fingerprint/Iris scan dobara
- Aadhaar print nikalwana

### 4. Documents check karna
Operator aapke documents check karta hai:
- Proof of Identity (POI): Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID
- Proof of Address (POA): Electricity bill, Ration card, Bank passbook
- DOB Proof: Birth certificate, SSC certificate

Documents theek nahi hain to kaam reject ho sakta hai.

### 5. Biometric process (agar required)
Fingerprints aur iris scan, aur photograph wahin lete hain.

### 6. Acknowledgement slip
Kaam hone ke baad Acknowledgement Receipt milta hai. Isme **Update Request Number (URN)** ya **Enrollment ID (EID)** hoti hai — isse online status dekh sakte hain.

### 7. Fees jama karna
- Correction: ₹50
- Mobile update: ₹50
- Aadhaar PVC card: ₹50

### 8. Status check aur follow-up
Online ya mAadhaar app se status check karein. 15 din ke baad bhi update nahi aaya to helpline ya dobara centre visit karna padta hai.
