# Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

## Keywords
APY, Atal Pension, pension, बुढ़ापा pension, retirement, NPS, unorganized worker pension, savings bank pension

## Purpose
Government-backed pension for Indian citizens aged 18-40 who don't pay income tax. Provides old-age income security for unorganized sector workers.

## Eligibility
- Age: 18 to 40 years
- Must not be income tax-payer (as of Oct 1, 2022)
- Must have a savings bank account
- Contributions continue till age 60 via auto-debit (monthly/quarterly/half-yearly)

## Benefits at Age 60
- Guaranteed monthly pension: ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 (based on contribution)
- Same pension to spouse after subscriber's death
- Lump sum to nominee after death of both subscriber and spouse

## Tax Benefit
Section 80CCD(1) deduction.

## Early Exit (Before 60)
Only own contributions + net interest returned (after charges). No return of govt co-contribution.

## Death Before 60
- Option 1: Spouse continues APY account till subscriber would have been 60
- Option 2: Full corpus returned to spouse/nominee
