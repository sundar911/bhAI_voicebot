# Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

## Keywords
PMMY, Mudra, मुद्रा, Mudra loan, business loan, छोटा loan, Shishu, Kishore, Tarun, micro enterprise, small business, बैंक loan

## Objective
Affordable credit up to ₹20 lakhs for micro/small enterprises in non-farm sectors (manufacturing, trading, services, allied agriculture like dairy, poultry, beekeeping).

## Loan Categories
- Shishu: up to ₹50,000
- Kishore: ₹50,001 – ₹5 lakh
- Tarun: ₹5,00,001 – ₹10 lakh
- Tarun Plus: ₹10 lakh – ₹20 lakh (existing Tarun borrowers with good repayment history)

## Eligible Borrowers
Individuals, Proprietors, Partnership Firms, Pvt. Ltd. or Public Companies. Must have good credit history and basic skill/experience.

## Where to Apply
Public/Private Sector Banks, Regional Rural Banks, Cooperative Banks, MFIs, NBFCs, Small Finance Banks.

## Interest Rate
As per bank norms (RBI guidelines). Shishu loans: processing fee usually waived. **No agents/facilitators authorized under PMMY.**

## Documents Required
- *For Shishu (up to ₹50,000)*: ID Proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID), Address Proof (utility bill/Aadhaar/passport), 2 passport photos, quotation of machinery, business registration (if any).
- *For Kishore/Tarun/Tarun Plus*: All above + 6-month bank statement, IT returns, balance sheets (if loan > ₹2 lakh), business plan, partnership deed/MoA (if applicable), net-worth statement.
