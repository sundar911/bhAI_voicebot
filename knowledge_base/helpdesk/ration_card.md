# Ration Card

## Common Questions & Answers

### "Who can apply?"
Indian citizens meeting eligibility criteria like income and family status.

### "Can I apply online?"
Yes, many states offer online applications via the Food and Civil Supplies website.

### "What are the types of ration cards?"
- **APL** (Above Poverty Line)
- **BPL** (Below Poverty Line)
- **AAY** (Antyodaya Anna Yojana)

### "What documents are required?"
ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN), address proof (Aadhaar, electricity bill), income proof, passport photos.

### "What is the processing time?"
Usually 15-30 days for verification and approval.

### "How to track my application?"
Check status on your state's Food and Civil Supplies website.

### "What can be updated?"
Name, DOB, address, family members, income category, photo, Aadhaar linkage.

### "Can I update online?"
Yes, visit your state's website, submit the request, upload documents.

### "Will verification happen?"
Yes, an official may visit your home for verification.

### "Can I update offline?"
Yes, visit your nearest Ration Office to submit the update request and documents.

## Required Documents

### For New Ration Card
- **Identity Proof** (any one): Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card
- **Address Proof** (any one): Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, Gas Bill
- **Date of Birth Proof** (for children): Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate
- **Income Proof** (for Yellow/Chevron card): Income Certificate from Tehsildar or Local Corporator
- **Family Proof**: Aadhaar Cards of all family members, Birth Certificates of children
- **Photographs**: 2 Passport Size Photos of Head of Family
- Carry original documents and 1 Xerox copy

### For Update/Correction
- **For Name/Gender Update**: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID
- **For Address Update**: Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, Gas Bill
- **For DOB Update**: Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate
- **For Income Category Change** (Yellow/Chevron): Income Certificate from Tehsildar/Local Corporator
- **Photographs**: 2 Passport Size Photos of Head of Family (if required)
- Carry original documents and 1 Xerox copy
