# Gazette — Name / DOB Change

## Keywords
Gazette, गजट, name change, नाम बदलना, naam change, marriage name change, surname change, DOB correction, date of birth correction, official name change, affidavit name change, newspaper notice

## What it is
A Gazette is an **official government publication** used to legally record changes such as name corrections, date-of-birth changes, or other notifications. Once published, the Gazette notification is **legally valid throughout India** and is the official proof for updating Aadhaar, PAN, school records, and other documents.

## When you need a Gazette
- Change of name (e.g. after marriage — optional, not compulsory)
- Surname change (after marriage, divorce, or otherwise)
- Correction of name spelling
- Correction of date of birth
- Multiple corrections together (name + DOB) — must be clearly mentioned in one affidavit/application

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar card
- Identity proof (PAN / passport / voter ID)
- Address proof
- School certificate / birth certificate (for DOB)
- **Affidavit** stating the reason for the change (mandatory for almost all changes)
- 2 to 4 passport-size photographs
- Newspaper publication clipping (see below)

## Process
1. **Affidavit** before a notary stating the change you want to make (with valid supporting proof for DOB changes).
2. **Newspaper publication** of the change in 2 newspapers (one English, one local Marathi) — this is **compulsory in most cases** before the Gazette can be published.
3. **Apply online** through the official Gazette website. For Maharashtra: `egazette.maharashtra.gov.in`. CSC visit not mandatory unless biometric verification is required.
4. **Pay the fee** online or at the designated treasury / payment portal.
5. **Track status** online on the Gazette website using the application number.
6. After approval, **download the Gazette PDF** from the official website.

## Fees & Timeline
- **Fee**: ₹100 to ₹500 (varies)
- **Time**: 30 to 60 days after approval (publication takes time)
- **Office hours**: 10:00 AM – 5:30 PM (Mon-Fri)

## After publication
Submit the Gazette notification to update Aadhaar, PAN, bank, school certificates, voter ID, and any other documents. Each agency has its own update process.

## Common Questions

### "Shadi ke baad name change Gazette mein karna compulsory hai?"
Nahin — it is optional. Karna ho to Gazette ke through officially update kar sakti ho.

### "DOB change ke liye kya chahiye?"
Affidavit + valid proof of correct birthdate (birth certificate, school cert, passport) + supporting documents. Submit with application.

### "Galat information print ho gayi to kaise theek karu?"
Submit a fresh application with correct details, affidavit, and supporting documents.

### "Online apply kar sakte hain?"
Haan — Maharashtra Gazette website pe online application accept hoti hai.

### "Newspaper mein notice dena zaroori hai?"
Haan, in most cases newspaper publication is compulsory before Gazette publication.

### "Old Gazette kaise dhundhu?"
Old Gazette numbers can be searched on the official Gazette portal or by applying at the Gazette office.

### "Ek hi application mein name aur DOB dono change ho sakte hain?"
Haan — but both must be clearly mentioned in the affidavit and application form.
