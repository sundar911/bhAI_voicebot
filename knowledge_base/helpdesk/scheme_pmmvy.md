# Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

## Keywords
PMMVY, Matru Vandana, मातृ वंदना, pregnant women, गर्भवती, lactating, maternity benefit, pregnancy, ₹5000 pregnancy, mother

## Objective
Maternity benefits for pregnant and lactating women from disadvantaged backgrounds.

## Benefits
- *First child (any gender)*: ₹5,000 in 2 instalments
  - ₹3,000 on pregnancy registration + 1 ANC check-up
  - ₹2,000 after childbirth registration + immunization till 14 weeks
  - Additional ₹1,000 under Janani Suraksha Yojana = ~₹6,000 total
- *Second child (only if girl)*: ₹6,000 in one instalment

## Eligibility
- Woman aged 19+
- Pregnant or lactating, facing wage loss
- Apply within 270 days of childbirth
- Must belong to: SC/ST, Disabled (40%+), or BPL Card holder

## Documents Required
Aadhaar (mapped to bank/post office), bank account details, eligibility proof, MCP/RCHI card, LMP date, ANC date, child birth certificate, child immunization details.

## Where to Check
pmmvy.wcd.gov.in — registration and instalment tracking.
