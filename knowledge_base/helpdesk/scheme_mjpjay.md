# Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY) – Maharashtra

## Keywords
MJPJAY, Mahatma Phule, ज्योतिराव फुले, Jan Arogya, Maharashtra health, ₹5 lakh Maharashtra, yellow ration card health, white ration card health, road accident insurance, राज्य health insurance

## Launched By
Govt. of Maharashtra (Public Health Dept.). Effective 1st July 2024. Coverage: entire population of Maharashtra.

## Key Benefits
- Categories A, B, C, E: ₹5 lakh per family per year (floater basis)
- Category D (Road Accident Victims): ₹1 lakh per person per year

## Medical Coverage
- A/B/C/E: 1356 packages across 34 specialties
- D: 184 packages (road accident-related)
- Follow-up: 262 packages
- Govt hospital exclusive: 119 packages

## Cashless Package Includes
Hospitalization (bed, nursing, doctor fees), oxygen, ICU, drugs, implants, blood transfusion, X-rays, tests, inpatient food, transport after discharge, body transport in case of death.

## Eligibility by Category
- A: Yellow/AAY/Annapurna/Orange ration cardholders
- B: White ration cardholders + Maharashtra domicile
- C: Students/orphans/women/elderly in Govt institutions, journalists, construction workers
- D: Road accident victims from outside Maharashtra/India
- E: Families from 865 villages near MH-Karnataka border (AAY, PHH, Annapurna)

## Valid ID Proofs
Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, DL, Passport, College ID, Health Card, Bank Passbook with Photo, Sr. Citizen Card, Handicap Certificate, Govt-issued photo ID.

## Where to Check
jeevandayee.gov.in — empanelled hospitals, procedures covered.
