# Income Certificate

## Keywords
income certificate, उत्पन्न प्रमाणपत्र, आय प्रमाण, salary certificate, BPL certificate, scholarship income proof, Talathi income, Tahsildar income, salary slip Form 16, land records farmer

## What it is
An income certificate is an official document that certifies an individual's or family's **annual income** from all sources. Used for scholarships, government schemes, fee concessions, caste certificates, ration card eligibility, loans, and various official purposes.

## Who Issues It
- **Tahsildar / Tehsildar** of the respective taluka or sub-division is the **final approving authority** who issues the certificate after verification.
- **Talathi** verifies ground-level details, collects documents, and prepares a report for the Tahsildar.
- The two offices work together through the e-Governance system.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar card
- Ration card
- Residence proof (electricity bill, voter ID, etc.)
- **For salaried**: Salary slip / Form 16 / employer's certificate
- **For farmers**: Land records (7/12, 8A) + Talathi's verification report on crop income
- Self-declaration of income
- Mobile number and email (for status updates and download links)

## Where to Apply
- **Online**: State portal (Aaple Sarkar / MahaOnline) — aaplesarkar.mahaonline.gov.in
- **Offline**: Talathi office, Tehsildar office, or Common Service Centre (CSC)
- When submitted via CSC, the application is forwarded online to Talathi (verification) and Tahsildar (approval).

## Verification Flow
1. Documents submitted (online or in person).
2. **Talathi** checks documents and conducts a local inquiry / field visit if needed.
3. Talathi prepares a report based on documents, official records, and possibly a visit.
4. **Tahsildar** reviews Talathi's report and cross-checks with other government databases.
5. Certificate issued and downloadable online.

## Office Hours
- Talathi & Tahsildar: 10:00 AM – 5:30 PM (Mon-Sat, except govt holidays)
- Lunch: 1:30 PM – 2:00 PM
- If Talathi office is on leave, another officer may be given temporary charge or the work redirected to the Tahsildar.

## Fees & Timeline
- **Fee**: ₹10 – ₹50 (varies by state)
- **Time**: 7 to 15 working days
- **Validity**: Usually **1 year** from the date of issue

## Common Questions

### "Income certificate kaun banata hai?"
Tahsildar approve karte hain. Talathi pehle verify karta hai aur report banata hai.

### "Salaried hu, kya documents chahiye?"
Salary slip, Form 16, employer ka certificate. Plus Aadhaar, ration card, residence proof.

### "Farmer hu, kya chahiye?"
Land records (7/12, 8A), Talathi ka verification report, Aadhaar, ration card.

### "Kitne din mein milta hai?"
7 se 15 working days.

### "Validity kya hai?"
Usually 1 saal.

### "Online apply kar sakti hu?"
Haan, state portal pe — Aaple Sarkar (Maharashtra). CSC se bhi online forward ho jata hai Talathi/Tahsildar ko.

### "Kya online download ho sakta hai certificate?"
Haan, most states mein application number se portal se download kar sakti ho.

### "Application reject ho gayi to reason kaise pata chalega?"
SMS, email, ya portal pe application status mein reason dikhta hai.

### "Kya personally jana padega?"
Most cases mein nahin if documents correct hain. Clarification ke liye presence required ho sakta hai.

### "Mistakes ho gayi documents mein, kaise theek karu?"
Corrected documents written request ke saath submit karo, ya correct details ke saath dobara apply karo.

## Notes
Maharashtra mein exact fee aur taluka-specific Talathi office addresses ke liye, मैं पूछ के बताती हूँ. District administration website pe addresses mil jaate hain.
