# NSAP — Central Pension Schemes (IGNOAPS, IGNWPS, IGNDPS, NFBS)

## Keywords
NSAP, IGNOAPS, IGNWPS, IGNDPS, NFBS, Indira Gandhi pension, old age pension, बुढ़ापा pension, widow pension, विधवा pension, disability pension, अपंग pension, family benefit, breadwinner death, मृत्यु sahayata

## Overview
Four central pension/assistance schemes under the National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP), targeted at BPL households. Application through Gram Panchayat / Municipality / Tehsildar; payments via DBT to bank account.

## IGNOAPS — Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme
- For BPL persons aged 60+
- ₹200/month (60-79 years), ₹500/month (80+)
- States usually add a top-up

## IGNWPS — Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme
- For BPL widows aged 40-79
- ₹300/month (Centre share); states add top-up
- After age 80, transitions to IGNOAPS

## IGNDPS — Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme
- For BPL persons aged 18-79 with severe/multiple disability (≥80%)
- ₹300/month (Centre share); states add top-up
- After 80, transitions to IGNOAPS

## NFBS — National Family Benefit Scheme
- One-time lump sum of **₹20,000** to a BPL family on death of the primary breadwinner (aged 18-59), regardless of cause
- Apply within 1 year of death
- Eligible kin: surviving head of household

## Documents Required (Common)
Aadhaar, BPL ration card / income certificate, age proof, bank passbook, passport-size photo, death certificate (NFBS), disability certificate (IGNDPS), spouse's death certificate (IGNWPS).

## Where to Apply
- Online: nsap.nic.in — application + beneficiary status
- Offline: Gram Panchayat / Municipal ward office / Tehsildar
