# Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana (Maharashtra)

## Keywords
Ladki Bahin, लाडकी बहीण, Majhi Ladki, Mukhyamantri Ladki, Maharashtra women scheme, ₹1500 women, बहीण scheme, women monthly, single woman pension, married woman scheme

## Objective
Direct monthly cash transfer of ₹1,500 to eligible women in Maharashtra for financial independence and household support.

## Eligibility
- Resident of Maharashtra
- Woman aged 21 to 65 years
- Married, widowed, divorced, abandoned, or unmarried (single woman from family)
- Annual family income up to ₹2.5 lakh
- Aadhaar-linked bank account in her own name
- Family does not have a 4-wheeler (other than tractor)
- No family member is income-tax payer or in regular govt service

## Benefit
₹1,500/month transferred directly to her bank account (DBT).

## Documents Required
Aadhaar, ration card, domicile certificate (Maharashtra), income certificate, marriage certificate (if married), bank passbook, passport-sized photo.

## Where to Check
ladkibahin.maharashtra.gov.in — registration and status. Also via Anganwadi worker, Setu Kendra, or CSC.

## Notes
