# Domicile Certificate

## Keywords
domicile, डोमिसाइल, अधिवास, permanent residence proof, state proof, Maharashtra domicile, scholarship eligibility, government job certificate, MahaOnline domicile

## What it is
A domicile certificate is an official document that proves a person's **permanent residence** in a particular state. Used for government jobs, education admissions, scholarships, and other official purposes.

## Eligibility
- Must have been residing in the state for the period specified by state rules (often 10-15 years for Maharashtra, with exceptions).
- Migrants from other states must prove long-term residence.
- A person born outside Maharashtra but who studied here long-term can usually be eligible.
- Parents/legal guardians can apply on behalf of minors.
- Widows, orphans, and children without parents can apply through a guardian, caretaker, or authority with valid documents.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar card (mandatory in most cases; other ID proofs may also be accepted)
- Residential proof: ration card, electricity bill, voter ID, property tax receipt, rent agreement
- School-leaving certificate (accepted as supporting proof)
- Birth certificate
- Affidavit (if needed)
- Mobile number linked for OTP verification

If name/address differs on Aadhaar, update Aadhaar first or submit additional supporting documents.

## Where to Apply
- **Online**: MahaOnline / Aaple Sarkar portal — aaplesarkar.mahaonline.gov.in
- **Offline**: Tehsildar office, Setu Kendra, or any Common Service Centre (CSC)
- Physical presence at the service centre is usually not required, but in some states biometric (fingerprint) verification is mandatory.

## Fees & Timeline
- **Fee**: ₹50–₹100 (varies by state)
- **Time**: 7-15 working days after applying
- **Validity**: Usually lifetime, unless the address changes

## Common Questions

### "Domicile aur Residence Certificate mein kya difference hai?"
Domicile proves **permanent residence** in a state; Residence Certificate proves **current address**. Domicile is typically issued once and valid lifetime; Residence is issued for ~1 year.

### "Domicile sirf government job ke liye chahiye?"
Nahin — it is also needed for education admissions, scholarships, govt schemes, and other benefits.

### "Application kahan se apply karu?"
Online via MahaOnline / Aaple Sarkar, or offline at Tehsildar office, Setu Kendra, or CSC.

### "Application kitne din mein milta hai?"
Usually 7 to 15 working days after submission, subject to verification.

### "Application reject ho gayi to dobara apply kar sakte hain?"
Haan, after correcting the mistakes or providing the correct documents.

### "Naam ya address mein galti hai to kaise theek karu?"
Submit a correction application with valid proof documents.

### "Status kaise check karu?"
Online on Aaple Sarkar portal using the application ID. SMS/email notifications also sent.
