# Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Anudan Yojana (Maharashtra)

## Keywords
Sanjay Gandhi, Niradhar, Anudan, संजय गांधी, निराधार, अनुदान, Maharashtra pension, destitute, विधवा pension, widow, divyang, transgender, HIV pension

## Objective
Monthly financial assistance for destitute Maharashtra residents with no livelihood.

## Eligibility
- Resident of Maharashtra, BPL category
- Women: 18-65 years; Men: 65+ years
- Includes: destitute widows, orphans, divorced women, disabled (40%+), transgender persons with no support, HIV/AIDS patients without income

## Benefits
- ₹600/month (single beneficiary)
- ₹900/month (family with 2+ beneficiaries)

## Documents Required
Domicile certificate (Maharashtra), income certificate, age proof (Aadhaar/birth certificate), disability certificate (if applicable), death certificate (if widow/orphan), divorce papers (if divorced), ration card, bank passbook, passport-sized photos.

## Where to Check
sjsa.maharashtra.gov.in (Social Justice & Special Assistance Dept)
