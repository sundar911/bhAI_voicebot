# Other Schemes — Brief Reference

## PMKVY — Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana
**Skill training & certification** for youth and workers. Free short-term training (200-600 hours) in 250+ job roles, certificate from National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). Apply at pmkvyofficial.org or any registered training centre.

## PMSBY — Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana
**Accident insurance** at ₹20/year auto-debited from bank account. Coverage: ₹2 lakh on accidental death or permanent total disability; ₹1 lakh on partial disability. Eligibility: 18-70 years with savings account. Enrol via your bank.

## PMJJBY — Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana
**Life insurance** at ₹436/year. Coverage: ₹2 lakh on death (any cause). Eligibility: 18-50 years with savings account; cover continues till age 55. Enrol via bank — annual auto-debit.

## JSY — Janani Suraksha Yojana
**Cash incentive for institutional delivery**. ₹600 (urban) / ₹700 (rural) for delivery in govt or accredited hospital. Eligibility: pregnant women (BPL/SC/ST or LPS states). Documents: JSY card, MCP card, bank passbook, Aadhaar.

## JSSK — Janani Shishu Suraksha Karyakram
**Free maternal & newborn care** at govt hospitals: free delivery (incl. C-section), free drugs, diagnostics, food during stay, free transport (home → hospital → home), free treatment of sick newborns up to 1 year. No out-of-pocket cost.

## MGNREGA
**100 days of guaranteed wage employment per year** for any rural household whose adult members volunteer for unskilled manual work. Apply for a job card at Gram Panchayat. Wages ₹250-300/day (varies by state). Online: nrega.nic.in. Largely rural — limited use for urban Mumbai artisans.

## Shravanbal Seva Rajya Nivruttivetan Yojana (Maharashtra)
**Maharashtra state pension for the elderly poor**. ₹1,000-1,500/month for residents aged 65+ from BPL families (for those covered, also IGNOAPS top-up). Apply at Tehsildar / Setu Kendra. Documents: domicile, age proof, BPL ration card, Aadhaar, bank passbook.

## Bal Sangopan Yojana (Maharashtra)
**Foster-care assistance** for children in vulnerable situations — orphans, single-parent, parent in prison, parent with terminal illness, abandoned. ₹2,250/month per child paid to the caregiver. Apply via Child Welfare Committee (CWC) / District Women & Child Development office.

## Manodhairya Yojana (Maharashtra)
**Compensation & support for women/children victims of rape, sexual assault, acid attack**. Lump-sum compensation (₹2-10 lakh based on case) + counselling + rehabilitation. Apply via Manodhairya committee at District level. Police FIR is the entry point.

## Annapurna Yojana
**10 kg free food grains/month** to destitute elderly (65+) who are eligible for IGNOAPS but not receiving any pension. Apply at the ration shop / Tehsildar. Documents: age proof, BPL ration card, Aadhaar.
