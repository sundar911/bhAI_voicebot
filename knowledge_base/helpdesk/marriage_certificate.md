# Marriage Certificate

## Keywords
marriage certificate, शादी certificate, विवाह प्रमाणपत्र, marriage registration, Hindu Marriage Act, Special Marriage Act, Sub-Registrar, witnesses, NRI marriage, foreign spouse, marriage age, surname change marriage, e-marriage certificate

## Common Questions & Answers

### "Who needs it?"
Anyone legally married in India who needs proof for legal, visa, or financial reasons.

### "Can I apply online?"
Yes, marriage registration can be initiated online. Visit your state's registration portal.

### "Do both partners need to be present?"
Yes, typically both partners need to be present at the registrar's office for registration.

### "What is the processing time?"
Varies by state — from a few days to a few weeks.

### "What if the marriage is not registered?"
An unregistered marriage may cause issues with legal documents, visas, and property claims. Registration is strongly recommended.

### "What about surname change?"
A gazette notification or updated Aadhaar/PAN after marriage can be used for surname change.

### "Lost certificate – what to do?"
Apply for a duplicate marriage certificate at the registrar's office where you registered.

### "What can be fixed in the certificate?"
Name, date of marriage, address, and other details can be corrected with supporting documents.

### "Can corrections be done online?"
Some corrections may be done online. Check your state's registration portal.

### "What documents are needed for correction?"
Supporting proof documents such as Aadhaar, birth certificate, and an affidavit may be required.

### "Marriage registration mandatory hai?"
Haan — Supreme Court ki direction ke according marriage registration mandatory hai, religion ke baghair.

### "Konse Acts ke under register hota hai?"
**Hindu Marriage Act 1955**: Hindus, Sikhs, Jains, Buddhists ke liye — already solemnized marriage register hota hai. **Special Marriage Act 1954**: kisi bhi do logon ki civil marriage (inter-faith bhi) — 30 din ka public notice required hota hai.

### "HMA aur SMA mein difference?"
HMA mein religious rites se shaadi pehle ho chuki hoti hai aur registration baad mein hota hai (kuch din mein). SMA civil marriage hai do persons ke beech, religion ke baghair, 30-day public notice ke saath.

### "Witnesses kitne chahiye?"
2 se 3 witnesses, valid ID proof ke saath (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID).

### "Min age?"
Groom ke liye 21 saal, bride ke liye 18 saal — Indian law ke according.

### "Kahan apply karu?"
Sub-Registrar / Marriage Registrar ka office (district mein), ya state government portal pe online.

### "Kitna time lagta hai?"
HMA: 7-30 din. SMA: 30-60 din (30-day public notice ki wajah se).

### "Fee kya hai?"
₹100 se ₹500 — state aur Act pe depend karta hai.

### "Donon partners ka aana zaroori hai?"
Haan — donon spouses aur witnesses ko appointment ke din Registrar ke office mein personally present hona padta hai.

### "NRI register kar sakte hain?"
Haan — passport copies, valid visa, aur required documents ke saath India mein register kar sakte hain.

### "Foreign spouse hai to?"
Special Marriage Act ke under register karo with foreign spouse ka passport, visa, aur unke embassy se No Objection Certificate (NOC).

### "Validity kya hai?"
Lifetime — India bhar mein aur internationally legal document ke roop mein accept hota hai.

### "Marriage certificate kahan kahan kaam aata hai?"
Passport, visa, spouse name change, insurance, joint bank account, inheritance claims — aur kai legal/official purposes.

### "Ek state ka certificate doosre state mein chalega?"
Haan — kisi bhi state ka issued Marriage Certificate poori India aur internationally bhi valid hai.

### "Online download ho sakta hai?"
Kai states mein approval ke baad e-Marriage Certificate official portal se download ho sakta hai.

## Required Documents

### For New Marriage Certificate
- **Identity Proof** of both partners: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card
- **Age Proof**: Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate
- **Address Proof**: Electricity Bill, Ration Card
- Passport-size photographs of both partners
- Wedding invitation card (if available)
- Two witnesses with identity proof

### For Update/Correction
- Supporting documents for the specific change (Name, Date, Address)
- Aadhaar Card of both partners
- Affidavit (if required by the registrar)
- Original marriage certificate
- Carry original documents and 1 Xerox copy of each
