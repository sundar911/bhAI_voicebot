# PM Vishwakarma Yojana

## Keywords
Vishwakarma, विश्वकर्मा, artisan scheme, कारीगर, traditional craft, hath का काम, toolkit, artisan loan, skill training artisan, certificate of recognition

## Objective
Support for traditional artisans and craftspeople — recognition, skill upgrade, toolkit incentive, and credit. Aimed at 18 traditional trades (e.g. tailor, cobbler, blacksmith, goldsmith, potter, basket weaver, doll-maker, garland-maker, washerman, fisherman, etc.).

## Key Benefits
- **Recognition**: PM Vishwakarma Certificate + ID Card after registration
- **Skill training**: Basic (5-7 days) + Advanced (15+ days), with stipend ₹500/day during training
- **Toolkit incentive**: ₹15,000 e-voucher to buy modern tools after basic training
- **Credit support**: Collateral-free enterprise loan
  - First tranche up to ₹1 lakh (18-month repayment)
  - Second tranche up to ₹2 lakh (30-month repayment) after good repayment
  - Concessional interest 5% (govt subsidy on top)
- **Digital incentive**: ₹1 per UPI transaction, up to 100/month
- **Marketing support**: Branding, e-commerce onboarding, trade fairs

## Eligibility
- Self-employed artisan in one of the 18 listed trades
- Age 18+ at registration
- Working in the trade as primary occupation
- Not availed similar credit-linked scheme from Centre/State in last 5 years (PMEGP, PMMY, Mudra, etc.)

## Documents Required
Aadhaar, mobile linked to Aadhaar, bank account, ration card, caste certificate (if applicable). Verification via Gram Panchayat / ULB.

## Where to Check
pmvishwakarma.gov.in — registration, trade eligibility, loan and toolkit details. Apply via CSC.
