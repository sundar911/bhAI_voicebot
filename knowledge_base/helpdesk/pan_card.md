# PAN Card

## Common Questions & Answers

### "Who can apply?"
Any Indian citizen, NRI, or foreigner doing business in India.

### "Can I apply online?"
You cannot get a PAN card instantly online. You need to prepare a few documents and visit a centre or contact Vijay or Priti for assistance.

### "How long does PAN delivery take?"
After successful application, a physical PAN card is delivered in approximately 15-20 working days.

### "How to track PAN status?"
Track your application on the NSDL or UTIITSL portal using your acknowledgment number.

### "Lost PAN card?"
Apply for a reprint or duplicate PAN card online at NSDL or UTIITSL portal. Mention 'Reprint of PAN' and provide your existing PAN number.

### "What details can be updated?"
Name, Father's Name, Date of Birth, Address, Photo, Signature, and Aadhaar linkage.

### "How to update PAN?"
Visit the NSDL or UTIITSL portal online, or visit a PAN centre with required documents. Vijay or Priti can also assist.

### "What documents are needed for update?"
Proof of identity and address relevant to the change. Aadhaar card is commonly required.

### "What is the update fee?"
Approximately ₹110 for Indian address, ₹1020 for foreign address.

### "Is e-PAN available?"
Yes, e-PAN (digital PAN) can be downloaded from NSDL or UTIITSL portal after your application is processed.

## Required Documents

### For New PAN Card
- **Identity Proof** (any one): Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License
- **Address Proof** (any one): Electricity Bill, Bank Passbook, Aadhaar Card
- **Date of Birth Proof** (any one): Birth Certificate, SSC Certificate, Aadhaar Card
- 2 Passport Size Photos
- Carry originals and 1 Xerox copy of each document

### For Update/Correction
- **Identity Proof** (Name/Father Name change): Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID
- **Date of Birth Proof** (DOB update): Birth Certificate, SSC Certificate
- **Address Proof** (address update): Utility bill, Bank Statement
- Carry originals and 1 Xerox copy of each document
