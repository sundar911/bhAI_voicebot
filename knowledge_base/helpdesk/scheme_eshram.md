# e-Shram Card

## Keywords
e-Shram, eshram, ई-श्रम, UAN card, unorganised worker, asanghatit, मज़दूर card, worker registration, labour card, unorganized labour database

## Objective
National database of unorganised workers — domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers, artisans, gig workers, agricultural labourers, migrant workers, and others not covered by EPF/ESIC.

## Key Benefits
- **Universal Account Number (UAN)**: 12-digit lifetime number
- **e-Shram card**: Identity for accessing govt welfare schemes
- **Accident insurance** (PMSBY-linked): ₹2 lakh death/permanent disability, ₹1 lakh partial disability
- **Single-window**: Registered workers easier to onboard for future welfare programs

## Eligibility
- Age 16 to 59 years
- Not member of EPF, ESIC, or NPS (govt employee/organized sector)
- Aadhaar mandatory; bank account in own name; mobile linked to Aadhaar

## Documents Required
Aadhaar, Aadhaar-linked mobile (for OTP), bank account number + IFSC.

## Where to Apply
- Online: eshram.gov.in (self-registration)
- CSC center
- State Seva Kendra

Free of cost. No agent fee.

## Notes
e-Shram card alone is not a scheme — it's a registration. The benefits come through other schemes (PMSBY, PM Vishwakarma, ration eligibility) that use e-Shram as the eligibility base.
