# Payroll & Salary

## Pay Cycle
- Salary har mahine ke last week ya 1st week me
- Bank transfer/UPI se aati hai
- Payslip milti hai - dekhna zaroori

## Wage Structure
- Daily wage basis with monthly payout
- Overtime sirf manager approval se; extra hours @ 1.5x rate
- Timing limit safety ke liye

## Deductions
- Late marks: 3 late = half-day cut
- Absent without info: full-day cut
- Advance recovery: EMI style monthly cut

## Bonuses
- Festival bonus (Diwali/Eid): performance + attendance par depend
- Quality bonus: agar defect rate low
- On-time bonus: agar attendance 100%

## Payslip Samajhna
- Gross wage: total earned
- Deductions: late/absent/advance
- Bonuses: quality/festival
- Net pay: final amount

Kisi ko bhi samajh na aaye toh impact team help karegi.

## Common Questions

### "Salary kyun kata?"
→ Late marks/absent without info/advance recovery. Exact detail payslip me hai; impact team samjhayegi.

### "Overtime milega?"
→ Sirf manager approval ke baad; 1.5x rate; timing limit safety ke liye.

### "PF/ESI hai kya?"
→ Current PoC: PF/ESI enrollment limited; eligibility aur process ke liye impact team se confirm. Agar info unclear ho toh escalate.
