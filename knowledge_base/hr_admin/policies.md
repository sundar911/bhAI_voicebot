# HR Policies

## Leave Types

### Sick Leave
- 6 din/year
- Doctor note encourage kiya jata hai but emergency me baad me de sakte ho
- Jaldi inform karo - call/WhatsApp/voice note

### Planned Leave
- Pehle se batakar, calendar me mark karo
- Festivals ke aas-paas workload high ho sakta hai
- Team lead se approval lo

### Emergency Leave
- Family medical, bachon ka school urgent kaam
- Bas jaldi se info do
- Impact team samjhegi

### Unpaid Leave
- Agar paid leave khatam ho jaye
- Team se discuss karke approve karwana
- Salary me deduction hoga

## Attendance Rules
- Morning roll-call at 9:30am
- Late after 9:45am → "late mark"
- 3 late marks = 1 half-day deduction
- Full day miss without info → full-day cut

## Common Questions

### "Kal nahi aa paungi"
→ Reason batao, leave type choose karo, team lead ko jaldi message karo.

### "Bina bataye chutti li thi"
→ Full-day deduction hoga. Aage se pehle batao, emergency me bhi message karo.
