# Government Schemes

## Financial Support

### Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
Business loans for micro/small enterprises (non-farm):
- Shishu: up to ₹50,000
- Kishore: ₹50,001–₹5 lakh
- Tarun: ₹5–10 lakh
- Tarun Plus: ₹10–20 lakh
Apply at: any bank, RRB, cooperative bank, MFI, NBFC, or small finance bank

### Atal Pension Yojana (APY)
Pension scheme for unorganized sector workers:
- Eligibility: age 18–40, non-income-tax-payer, has a savings account
- Benefit: ₹1,000–₹5,000/month guaranteed pension from age 60
- On death: spouse gets same pension; then lump sum to nominee
- Tax benefit: Section 80CCD(1)

### Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)
Savings scheme for girl child:
- Eligibility: girl child below 10 years old; max 2 accounts per family
- Interest: ~8% tax-free; maturity at 21 years or marriage after 18
- Min deposit: ₹250/year; max: ₹1.5 lakh/year
- Partial withdrawal (50%) after girl turns 18 for education

### Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Anudan Yojana (Maharashtra)
Monthly support for destitute Maharashtra residents:
- Eligibility: BPL, women 18–65 or men 65+; includes widows, disabled (40%+), divorced, HIV+ with no income
- Benefit: ₹600/month (individual), ₹900/month (family 2+)

## Health Schemes

### Ayushman Bharat – PM-JAY
Cashless hospitalization up to ₹5 lakh/family/year:
- Covers secondary & tertiary care, pre-existing conditions from Day 1
- Urban eligibility: artisans, street vendors, construction workers, drivers, domestic workers automatically eligible
- No premium; fully government-funded

### Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)
Maternity benefit for women 19+:
- 1st child: ₹5,000 in 2 instalments (+ ₹1,000 from JSY = ₹6,000 total)
- 2nd child (if girl): ₹6,000 in one instalment
- Apply within 270 days of childbirth

### Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY) — Maharashtra
Cashless hospitalization for entire Maharashtra population:
- Coverage: ₹5 lakh/family/year (1356 packages, 34 specialties)
- Category A (Yellow/AAY/Annapurna/Orange ration card): automatically covered
- Category B (White ration card + Maharashtra domicile): covered

## Livelihood

### Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – NRLM (DAY-NRLM)
Poverty reduction through women's Self-Help Groups (SHGs):
- Links one woman per rural poor household to an SHG
- SHGs get bank linkages, revolving fund, interest subvention
- Skill training and placement for rural youth

### Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U)
Subsidized housing for urban poor:
- EWS (income ≤ ₹3L), LIG (₹3–6L), MIG I (₹6–12L), MIG II (₹12–18L)
- Credit-linked subsidy on home loans
- Eligibility: no pucca house anywhere in India; no prior govt housing scheme
