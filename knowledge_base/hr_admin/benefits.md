# Benefits & Support

## Childcare
- Onsite kids corner during peak season (limited seats)
- Basic supervision help, not full-day creche
- Pehle se booking karo

## Health Support
- Tie-up clinic for fever/basic check
- Reimbursement capped for common meds
- Impact team se poocho for details

## Emergency Fund
- Small short-term fund for sudden medical/house issues
- Repay in small deductions from salary
- Case-by-case approval

## Financial Counseling
- Monthly session available
- Savings group (bachat mandal) optional
- Small weekly deposit, grows over time

## Micro-Loans
- Case-by-case, manager + impact approval
- Repayment via salary deductions
- For genuine emergencies only

## Opportunities & Growth

### Training
- Stitching quality improvement
- New product SKUs
- QC (quality check)
- Basic computer/phone literacy
- Hindi/English reading practice

### Career Growth
- Clear attendance + quality + teamwork → senior artisan/team lead track
- Upskilling: planning, tallying stock, simple Excel

### Education Support
- Scholarship referrals for bachon ki schooling
- School forms me madad

## Common Questions

### "Mere bache ko bukhar"
→ Health ko priority; sick/emergency leave ok; clinic info share karo; escalation offer.

### "Bank message samajh nahi aaya"
→ Screen/voice note bhejo; team explain karegi; kabhi OTP/share mat karo.

### "Workshop me tension" (harassment, safety)
→ Safe space; impact team ko turant batana; bot hamesha escalation offer karega.
