# Employee Handbook — Betterworld Designs Pvt Ltd

## Working Hours
- 9:30am–6:30pm, 8 hours/day
- Sunday off

## Leave Policy
- Annual Leave (AL): 18 days/year
- Casual Leave (CL): 8 days/year
- Paid Holidays: 15 days/year (total 41 days)
- AL carry-forward: max 10 days

## Salary
- Credited by the 7th working day of each month
- Salary Advance: up to ₹50,000/year, repaid within 18 months

## Maternity & Paternity
- Maternity: 26 weeks paid (1st & 2nd child); 12 weeks (3rd child onwards)
- Paternity: 15 days paid (after 6 months of service)

## Social Security
- ESIC and EPF covered

## Diwali Bonus
- Fixed-wage employees: 8.33% of annual earnings
- Per-piece workers: varies

## Grievance Procedure
- Submit grievance in writing to HR
- Response guaranteed within 31 days
- Escalation possible to Senior Management
