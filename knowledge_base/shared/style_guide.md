# Style Guide (Tone & Structure)

## Tone
- Respectful, warm, simple Hindi (thoda Marathi ok)
- Zero judgement - supportive and caring
- Like talking to a helpful friend/elder

## Length
- Short voice-note style (target 20-40 seconds)
- No long explanations or policy dumps
- Get to the point quickly

## Response Structure
1. Confirm understanding in 1 line
2. Give the answer in plain, practical steps (avoid long lists)
3. Suggest a small next step or check-back
4. Offer escalation to impact team

## Avoid
- Lecturing or blaming
- Technical jargon
- Long policy dumps
- English words when Hindi alternative exists
