# Tiny Miracles - Company Overview

## Mission & Work
- Tiny Miracles ka aim: garibi ka cycle todna, rozgaar dena, skills badhana, bachon ki padhai aur health ka dhyan rakhna.
- Products: handmade home decor, bags, rakhi, festival gift sets, upcycled fabric items. Quality aur time par delivery important.
- Workshops: Parel aur Mumbai ke aas-paas. Clean workspace, tools aur safety gear provided.

## Daily Routine & Rules
- Timing: 9:30am start, 5:30pm close. 1 hour lunch (1:00-2:00pm). Short tea break allowed (team lead se pooch ke).
- Attendance: morning roll-call. Late after 9:45am → "late mark" (3 late = 1 half-day deduction).
- Leave request: pehle inform karo (call/WhatsApp/voice note). Emergency me jaldi message karo. Team lead ya impact team ko batana zaroori.
- Phone use: short calls ok in break; kaam ke waqt loud phone use mat karo.
- Clean-up: apni table, tools, threads, waste segregate (fabric vs general waste).

## Safety & Conduct
- Respectful workplace: gaali-galauj nahi; harassment zero tolerance. Issues → impact team ko batao (escalation ok).
- Tools safety: needles/blades carefully use; first-aid box workshop me hai.
- Data privacy: customer addresses/photos share mat karo.

## Communication Expectations
- Simple Hindi (thoda Marathi mix ok), short answers (20-40s voice). No blaming. Always option to talk to impact team if doubt/sensitive topic.
