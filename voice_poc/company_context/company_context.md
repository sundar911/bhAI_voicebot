# Tiny Miracles – Company Context (PoC)

## Mission & Work
- Tiny Miracles ka aim: garibi ka cycle todna, rozgaar dena, skills badhana, bachon ki padhai aur health ka dhyan rakhna.
- Products: handmade home decor, bags, rakhi, festival gift sets, upcycled fabric items. Quality aur time par delivery important.
- Workshops: Parel aur Mumbai ke aas-paas. Clean workspace, tools aur safety gear provided.

## Daily Routine & Rules
- Timing: 9:30am start, 5:30pm close. 1 hour lunch (1:00–2:00pm). Short tea break allowed (team lead se pooch ke).
- Attendance: morning roll-call. Late after 9:45am → “late mark” (3 late = 1 half-day deduction).
- Leave request: pehle inform karo (call/WhatsApp/voice note). Emergency me jaldi message karo. Team lead ya impact team ko batana zaroori.
- Phone use: short calls ok in break; kaam ke waqt loud phone use mat karo.
- Clean-up: apni table, tools, threads, waste segregate (fabric vs general waste).

## Leave Types
- Sick leave: 6 din/year. Doctor note encourage kiya jata hai but emergency me baad me de sakte ho.
- Planned leave: pehle se batakar, calendar me mark karo. Festivals ke aas-paas workload high ho sakta hai.
- Emergency leave: family medical, bachon ka school urgent kaam. Bas jaldi se info do.
- Unpaid leave: agar paid leave khatam ho jaye, team se discuss karke approve karwana.

## Pay & Deductions
- Pay cycle: salary har mahine ke last week ya 1st week me bank transfer/UPI. Payslip milti hai.
- Daily wage basis with monthly payout. Overtime sirf manager approval se; extra hours @ 1.5x.
- Attendance link: full day miss without info → full-day cut. 3 late marks = half-day cut.
- Bonus: festival bonus (Diwali/Eid) performance + attendance par depend. Quality bonus agar defect rate low.
- Payslip samajh: gross wage, deductions (late/absent), bonuses, net pay. Kisi ko bhi samajh na aaye toh impact team help karegi.

## Support & Benefits
- Childcare: onsite kids corner during peak season (limited seats). Basic help, not full-day creche.
- Health: tie-up clinic for fever/basic check. Reimbursement capped for common meds (ask impact team).
- Emergency fund: small short-term fund for sudden medical/house issues; repay in small deductions.
- Financial counseling: monthly session. Savings group (bachat mandal) optional; small weekly deposit.
- Micro-loans: case-by-case, manager + impact approval; repayment via salary deductions.

## Opportunities & Growth
- Training: stitching quality, new product SKUs, QC, basic computer/phone literacy, Hindi/English reading practice.
- Upskilling: team leads coach on planning, tallying stock, simple Excel after basic training.
- Promotions: clear attendance, quality, teamwork → senior artisan/team lead track.
- Education support: scholarship referrals for bachon ki schooling, school forms me madad.

## Safety & Conduct
- Respectful workplace: gaali-galauj nahi; harassment zero tolerance. Issues → impact team ko batao (escalation ok).
- Tools safety: needles/blades carefully use; first-aid box workshop me hai.
- Data privacy: customer addresses/photos share mat karo.

## Common Situations (FAQs)
- “Salary kyun kata?” → late marks/absent without info/advance recovery. Exact detail payslip me hai; impact team samjhayegi.
- “Kal nahi aa paungi” → reason batao, leave type choose karo, team lead ko jaldi message karo.
- “Mere bache ko bukhar” → health ko priority; sick/emergency leave ok; clinic info share karo; escalation offer.
- “Bank message samajh nahi aaya” → screen/voice note bhejo; team explain karegi; kabhi OTP/share mat karo.
- “PF/ESI hai kya?” → Current PoC: PF/ESI enrollment limited; eligibility aur process ke liye impact team se confirm. Agar info unclear ho toh escalate.
- “Overtime milega?” → sirf approval ke baad; 1.5x rate; timing limit safety ke liye.
- “Workshop me tension” (harassment, safety) → safe space; impact team ko turant batana; bot hamesha escalation offer karega.

## Communication Expectations
- Simple Hindi (thoda Marathi mix ok), short answers (20–40s voice). No blaming. Always option to talk to impact team if doubt/sensitive topic.
